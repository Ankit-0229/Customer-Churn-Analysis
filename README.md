##  Key Insights

* **26.54%** of customers churned â€” a significant retention challenge.
* **Short-tenure (<3 months)** customers are at highest risk of leaving.
* **Month-to-month contracts** have the most churn; long-term plans retain better.
* **Electronic check** payment users churn the most â€” convenience issue.
* **High monthly charges** correlate with churn, indicating price sensitivity.
* Customers **without OnlineSecurity or TechSupport** are more likely to leave.

---

##  Recommendations

* Strengthen onboarding for first 3 months to reduce early churn.
* Offer loyalty discounts for annual or biannual contracts.
* Reassess pricing for high-charge customers.
* Bundle OnlineSecurity and TechSupport for better value.
* Promote digital payments to replace electronic check users.
